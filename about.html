About Us

AMJAD Integrated Service is a fully accredited JAMB CBT Centre providing reliable exam registration, IT training, and comprehensive computer classes. Our environment is tailored for students and professionals seeking excellence in technology.

What We Offer

JAMB CBT registration & mock exams

Internship programs for IT students

Practical computer classes for all levels

Our Mission

To empower learners with practical digital skills and support students to succeed in their JAMB exams through smart, tech-enabled learning systems.

Our Vision

To become a nationwide leader in digital learning and computer-based testing, known for innovation, quality, and accessibility.
